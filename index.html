Marilyn Monroe

An American actress, comedienne, singer and model.

Norma Jeane Mortenson—who will become better known around the world as the glamorous actress and sex symbol

Marilyn Monroe, born Norma Jeane Mortenson, was a globally recognized American actress, singer, and model. She became synonymous with the Hollywood sex symbol and a cultural icon during the 1950s and early 1960s, known for her films like "Gentlemen Prefer Blondes" and "Some Like It Hot". While her fame was immense, Monroe also faced personal struggles and died tragically at a young age.


Here is a time line of Marilyn Monroe's life:

  • 1926 - Born in Losangeles City Hospital
  • 1944 - Working in a factory as part of the war effort
  • 1950 - She became one of the most popular sex symbols
  • 1953 - One of the most marketable Hollywood stars, with leading role in three films: the noir Niagara、Gentlemen Prefer Blondes and How to Marry a Millionaire
  • 1954 - Returned to star in one of the biggest box office successes of her career, Seven Year Itch
  • 1955 - She dedicated to building her company and began studying method acting at the Actors Studio
  • 1956 - Got a critically acclaimed performance in Bus Stop
  • 1957 - Actinng in the first independent production of MMP, The Prince and the Showgirl
  • 1959 - She won a Golden Globe for Best Actress for Some Like It Hot
  • 1961 - Her last completed film was the drama The Mistits
  • 1962 - She died at the age of 36 on August 5, 1962 from an overdose of barbiturates at her home in Los Angeles.
